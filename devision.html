Bio Coal Briquettes & Pellets

A N R Global Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in the manufacturing of eco-friendly bio coal briquettes and biomass pellets, supporting sustainable and renewable energy solutions for industries around the world.

With modern production facilities and strict quality parameters, we deliver consistent and high-performance biomass fuel for both domestic and international markets.

Sustainable Energy Partner

Helping industries move towards low-carbon, renewable fuel alternatives.

  • 100% natural & carbon-neutral.
  • High calorific value.
